## Future Directions

The following areas may be addressed in subsequent versions:

### Version 1.1 — Extensions

- Information object definitions
- Extended relationship semantics
- Enhanced provenance mechanisms

### Version 2.0 — Advanced Features

- Distributed information architectures
- Semantic interoperability frameworks
- Cross-domain application profiles

### Working Groups

As UIS matures, specialized working groups may be established to address domain-specific applications:

- Healthcare information representation
- Legal and regulatory documentation
- Scientific data structures
- Financial information standards

## Participation

The timeline above is indicative and may be adjusted based on community input and resources. We encourage active participation in shaping the direction and priorities of UIS.
