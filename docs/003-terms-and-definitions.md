# Terms and Definitions

## Purpose

This document establishes the vocabulary and terminology used throughout the Universal Information Standard (UIS).

## Scope

The scope addresses:

- Terms and definitions essential to understanding UIS
- Abbreviations and acronyms
- Naming conventions
- Terminology sources and harmonization

## Content to be developed

- Fundamental term definitions
- Information model terminology
- Relationship terminology
- Provenance terminology
- Versioning terminology
- Abbreviations and acronyms
- Cross-reference to related standards (ISO 704)
