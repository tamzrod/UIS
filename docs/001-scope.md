# Scope

## Purpose

This document defines the field of application, objectives, and boundaries of the Universal Information Standard (UIS).

## Scope

The scope addresses:

- What UIS covers and its intended applications
- Objectives and goals of the specification
- What is intentionally excluded from UIS
- Relationship between UIS and domain-specific standards
- Stability and evolution policies

## Content to be developed

- Field of application statement
- Primary objectives and use cases
- Out-of-scope declarations
- Use case examples
- Stability and versioning policies
